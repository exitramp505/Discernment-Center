Open Bible Statement of Faith

Our Core Convictions About Church Multiplication

  1. We multiply churches to make disciples. Our mission is not to gather Christians from other churches, but to reach those who do not yet know Jesus. We are committed to seeing the lost saved, discipled, and brought into the family of God. Every church we plant is focused on engaging people far from faith and guiding them into a transformative relationship with Christ.
  2. We multiply churches that multiply. Healthy churches reproduce. Our church plants intentionally raise up new leaders, invest in the Open Bible Church Multiplication Collective, help host church planting events, and mentor other church multipliers. Multiplication is not an afterthought. It is an expectation.
  3. We multiply churches that experience God. Knowing about God is not enough. We want to encounter Him. Our church plants embrace the work of the Holy Spirit and equip people to minister in ways that are both healthy and fruitful. We believe that a thriving church is marked by both sound teaching and Spirit led transformation.
  4. We multiply churches that are unique in expression but unified in practice. Every church will be rooted in key biblical practices while expressing its mission in ways that fit its local context. These churches will gather regularly, teach the scriptures, practice communion and baptism, have qualified leadership, affiliate with Open Bible, contribute to the movement, and stay committed to outreach and evangelism.
